FluentSpeech: Stutter-Oriented Automatic Speech Editing with Context-Aware Diffusion Models

We demonstrate experimental results on two tasks (speech editing and stutter-oriented automatic speech editing also abbreviated as SASE) for demo respectively. For speech editing task, we provide 5 samples for each edit option, including insertion, replacement and deletion. For the insertion and replacement , we provide the original audio, the edited audio from baseline systems and our proposed system to compare the edit performance of different systems. In particular, we do not show the effect of delete operation, as difference between different system is not noticeable. For SASE task, we provide 10 samples including the original audio, the edited audio from the proposed system to show the high efficiency of our model in improving the fluency of stuttering speech.

Note: Audio samples include both seen and unseen speakers.
